MotherNets enables rapid training of diverse ensembles of deep neural networks: This it achieves by capturing the structural similarity that exists between neural networks in an ensemble and training for it only once. This demo shows you the benefit of MotherNets by plotting pre-trained ensemble of DCNNs.
